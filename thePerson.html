The Person

Syed Irfan was born in Chittagong, the port city of Bangladesh. He has a diverse background in education and experience in the field of software development and machine learning. He completed his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering from North South University after finishing his secondary schooling from Nasirabad Government High School and higher secondary school certification from Bangladesh Navy Colege, Chittagong.

During his professional career, Syed Irfan worked as a Software Engineer at Codephilics, a software startup company, where he faced the challenges that come with starting a company and persevered through obstacles. Additionally, he has been an official member of Toastmasters Internaional and has participated in their regular programs, earning certification as an active charter member of North South University Toastmasters Club. He has also been a member of North South University ACM SIGAPP Student Chapters and North South University Problem Solvers.

Currently, Syed Irfan is pursuing his Masters in Data Science at the University of Greenwich in London, beginning in Spring 2022. His interests include the fields of Data Science, Analytics, and Machine Learning and has a particular passion for Python programming and data analysis and visualization. He believes in making data-driven decisions to optimize business models and empower people for better lifestyles. In his free time, Syed Irfan also enjoys exploring his interests in Finance, Cryptography, Astronomy, and Human Psychology.
